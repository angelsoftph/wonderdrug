The Process

We prioritize having the shortest supply & manufacturing chains possible and maintaining ethical production standards.

Learn More

  • 01.

    Harvesting

    Ingredients are grown in fertile soil in central Thailand – then harvested and transferred to our facility where they are cleaned and prepared for processing.

  • 02.

    Processing

    Ingredients arrive at the manufacturing facility clean of soil and debris. They are sent through a washing process and staged for juicing or for the puree line.

  • 03.

    Bottling

    Product is bottled into PET #1 plastic bottles and capped, then rinsed with an organic sanitizing solution. PET plastic is the most widely recycled.

  • 04.

    HPP

    Products are sent through the HPP process – which involves the application of pressure (600 BAR), for about 2 minutes. The process crushes any potential bacteria or foodborne.

  • 05.

    Freeze

    After HPP, the bottles are air-dried and carted into the blast freezer where they are frozen solid within hours. Once frozen, the bottles are boxed and prepared for loading.

  • 06.

    Deliver

    Products are delivered either to our distribution partner or directly to the customers' home.Customer receives the bottle and stored it in the refrigerator before drinking it.
